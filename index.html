A little bit about me.

I’ve made my home base in the Greater Boston area. My journey into tech started with working as a systems administrator. As I began doing more with technology and learned to code, I realized how much I enjoyed designing & developing.

After building my first website, I realized I found something that gave me the freedom & versatility I was looking for in my work. Now I’ve made a full switch to front-end development, where I can use my organization skills and eye for detail to write clean, elegant code.

Hand-Coded HTML

My focus is writing clean, well-formatted, semantic HTML5 by hand to make sure that the content is easy to read, easy to collaborate, trouble-shoot and accessible.

Well-Organized CSS

I pride myself on writing CSS that is easy to read and build on. I focus on keeping my CSS lean and fast to load, and I make it a habit to stay up to date on current best practices.

Ease Converting Designs into Code

You can trust me to take a designer’s PSD and quickly & accurately convert it into a webpage that is pixel-perfect match.
